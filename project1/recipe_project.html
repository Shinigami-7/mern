Sponge cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour and baking powder.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add the milk to the egg mixture and mix well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let it cool before serving. Enjoy!
